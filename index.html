Unit Conversions
Temperature



To convert celsius to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsius: (C × 9/5) + 32
Temperature



To convert fahrenheit to celsius yourself, use this formula replacing the `F` with your temperature in fahrenheit: (F − 32) × 5/9
Weight



To convert pounds to kilograms yourself, use this formula replacing the `pounds` with your weight in pounds: pounds ÷ 2.205
Weight



To convert kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `kg` with your weight in kilograms: kg x 2.205
Distance



To convert kilometers to miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `km` with your distance in kilometers: km ÷ 1.609
Distance



To convert miles to kilometers yourself, use this formula replacing the `miles` with your distance in miles: miles x 1.609
